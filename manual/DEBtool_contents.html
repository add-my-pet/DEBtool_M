Contents

The routines are organized in the subdirectories (here called "toolboxes")
  • lib: general purpose routines,
    called by other routines in sister subdirectories
    regression routines
  • enzyme: enzyme kinetics
    compares enzyme performance in reactions between two complementary compounds
  • microbe: micro-organisms in reactors
    1 reserve, 1 structure, V1-morph
  • alga: nutrient-limited growth of algae in reactors
    more reserves, 1 structure, V1-morph
  • animal: animal physiology
    1 reserve, 1 structure, isomorph (iso_11)
  • iso_21: animal physiology
    2 reserves, 1 structure, isomorph
  • plant: plant physiology
    6 reserves, 2 structures, generalized morphology
  • community: 1 species community of mixotrophs
    0,1,3 reserves, 1 structure, V1-morph
  • symbi: interaction between hosts and symbionts in reactors
    host: 1 reserve, 1 structure, V1-morph
    symbiont: 2 reserves, 1 structure, V1-morph
  • tox: toxicokinetics, and effects of chemical compounds on organisms and populations
  • degrad: biodegradation of chemical compounds in batch cultures
  • fig_3: script files for figures of the DEB-book third edition
